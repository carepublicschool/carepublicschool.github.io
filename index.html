About Us

Care Public School, located in Narasaraopet, Palnadu District, Andhra Pradesh, is an English-medium institution offering classes from LKG to 10th grade. We believe in providing a balanced education focused on academics, character, and creativity.

We also provide hostel services for students who require accommodation, ensuring a safe and conducive living environment that complements their academic journey.

At Care Public School, we go beyond teaching curriculum subjects. We are committed to instilling discipline and motivation in our students, empowering them to grow personally and serve their communities. Our holistic approach prepares students to achieve their academic goals while developing the adaptability needed to thrive in dynamic situations and evolving generations.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that nurtures intellectual curiosity and holistic development. Our qualified teachers ensure strong foundations in English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies, along with extracurricular activities and values-based education.
